## What This Image Shows

Full-body character design sheet for Dr. Garoussen. Two-view turnaround (front and back — no side view). He is tall and lean with white/grey slicked-back hair, a red cybernetic eye (right eye), and a metal/cybernetic chin implant. He wears a dark navy military-style coat with a red diagonal sash and black trousers and shoes. Hands clasped behind his back in the back view. Pantone color swatches included.

Design sheet metadata: Season 01, Episode 01 "Pilot." ID: DS_villians_antagonist_DrGaroussen. Notes on sheet: "He has a bionic eye and a metal chin. Age: 67 years old. Height: 185 cm."

*Note on file naming:* The filename uses "Garousen" (one 's'); the canonical spelling is **Garoussen** (two 's'). The filename is a typo.

## Production Notes

Color palette is predominantly dark navy and black (Pantone 532C, 7544C) with red accents (Pantone 1925C, 1955C) matching the Futurum organization's color identity. The red diagonal sash is a recurring visual identifier across all colored comic pages. In colored pages, Garoussen is also identified as **"Dr. Elroy Garoussen 'Le Loup'"** — "Le Loup" (French for "The Wolf") is a nickname; "Elroy" is his first name as depicted in production materials.
