1. GIT LFS MIGRATION
================================================================================

Problem: Two files already in the repo exceeded GitHub's 50MB recommendation
(teaser trailer at 83MB, pitch deck at 58MB), with the hard 100MB limit at risk
as the project grows.

Solution: Configured Git Large File Storage (LFS) for all binary asset types.

- Initialized Git LFS in the repository
- Configured .gitattributes to track: *.mp4, *.pptx, *.pdf, *.jpg, *.png,
  *.mov, *.zip
- Migrated all 65 existing binary files (206 MB total) from regular git
  objects to LFS pointers
- All future files of those types are automatically tracked by LFS
- Pushed cleanly with no size warnings

Effect: Repo history stays lean permanently. No more large file warnings.
New assets (video, images, documents) can be added freely without hitting
GitHub's hard limits.

5. EPISODE 5 OUTLINE CREATED
================================================================================

File: create/outlines/ch05-outline.md
Title: "SIGNAL"
Status: In Progress (outline complete; script not started)
Chapter tracker updated to reflect outline status.

Built from:
  - E4 cliffhanger: K0fuji's email to Fr0g6Y ("I have info on the IDEA
    scientists. Meet me in the city.")
  - E4 cliffhanger: Helen detecting Marty bypassed her defenses
  - E4 thread: Coover/Lamarr investigation, Mitch identified from footage
  - E4 thread: Dasha's nano-swarm destabilization incident
  - E4 setup: Mitch designated as de facto leader by Helen
  - Ongoing: Chandra's unexplored role and capabilities

EPISODE STRUCTURE:

  Cold Open: Marty re-reads K0fuji's email in the dark. Helen's orb pauses
  outside his door. Moves on.

  Act One (Lab — The Decision):
    Marty shows the group the email. Tony: "So we're finding mom, right?"
    Helen objects — firmly and correctly. Mitch, as new leader, makes the
    call: small group goes. Mitch, Marty, Tony into the city. Ren, Dasha,
    Chandra stay with Helen. Tension in the split. Ren quietly loads a
    compact drone package into Mitch's wrist before they leave.

  Act Two (City — The Meeting):
    First ground-level look at the stratified city. Parallel thread: Coover
    and Lamarr are in the city closing in on a lead. The meet point: a noodle
    bar in the lower city. Twenty minutes of waiting. K0fuji arrives — teenager,
    nondescript, clearly done their research.
    K0fuji's intel: scientists are alive, being held underground east of the
    city, being forced to work on something. One more thing: "The man running
    the op doesn't answer to Futurum. He answers to someone who's supposed
    to be dead." Marty goes still.
    The abort: K0fuji spots surveillance. They scatter. Foot chase through
    a crowded market district. Coover spots a blue-haired kid with red glasses
    slipping into an alley — first physical sighting.
    Mitch deploys Ren's drone package (sensor blind, 90 seconds). They reach
    TRIX-E and go.
    Parallel lab thread: Dasha's nano-swarm moves without her tool. Ren
    witnesses it. Neither says anything.

  Act Three (Return — Consequences):
    Debrief. Helen asks only: "Did anyone follow you back?" The information
    K0fuji gave raises stakes — the scientists are working, not just held.
    Helen pauses too long when Mitch asks about "the dead man." She moves on.
    Marty, alone: types a reply to K0fuji. "Who is the dead man?"
    Chandra has spent the episode mapping unexplored lab sections with ROBI.
    Helen sees the map and asks: "Have you been in the north corridor?"
    Final beat: Helen alone in a hidden room of active screens.
    One screen shows a face. She whispers: "Not yet."

KEY THREADS ADVANCED:
  - K0fuji: "someone supposed to be dead" — first thread toward Supreme Leader
    reveal; Marty heard it
  - Coover: first physical sighting of Marty (blue hair, glasses)
  - Dasha: nano-swarm follows her without tool — Ren witnessed, neither
    has spoken
  - Materializer: Ren's drone package used by Mitch — extends "expertise
    channeling" to another character
  - Helen: private monitoring room revealed; she knows something she's
    not telling

OPEN QUESTIONS FLAGGED FOR AUTHOR (must resolve before scripting):
  1. Who is K0fuji physically? How do they know Fr0g6Y?
  2. What is in Helen's private room? What face is on the screen?
  3. Does Marty already suspect his father is alive?

    pitch materials, character designs, comic pages.

    Registration of Unpublished Works.
