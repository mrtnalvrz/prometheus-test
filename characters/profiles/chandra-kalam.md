## Personality

Adventurous and energetic, with zero self-pity. When bullied by Christy's group, she shows no sadness — she cheerfully invites them to join her game. She processes the world through fantasy: her stuffed dragon BiBi has conversations, her green army man runs campaigns, and the secret lab robots are just "Bubbies." This is not naivety — it is a fully developed alternative framework for understanding the world that turns out to be more useful than expected.

## Background

Her parents, Dr. Resha and Dr. Vaib Kalam, work on nanotechnology at IDEA. Not wanting to overwhelm her, they described their work as magic: robots became giant puppets, rocket science became mystic runes, particle accelerators became mana pools. Chandra grew up with an extraordinary imagination rooted in real science she doesn't yet know she understands. She is an only child — meeting Dasha was the most important event in her life.

## Possessions

- **BiBi** — her stuffed dragon (male). Beloved companion. She voices him as a proxy for her own feelings. Nearly destroyed by Christy's scissors.
- **The Green Army Man** — a small soldier she deploys in sandbox battles.

## Key Story Moments (Episodes 1–4)

- Bullied by Christy's group; saved by Dasha's flying kick off the school wall
- Rescued alongside Dasha when Ren destroys the targeting drone on her moped
- In the lab, recognizes "081" as "Oby" and reactivates R081 just by calling its name
- Renames R081 → **ROBI** by writing a red "R" in front of "081" with a sharpie — becomes his registered prime owner
- Discovers the control room by following a light down a dark corridor
- Her "OPEN SEZAMI!" spell coincides with Marty's silent hack; he lets her believe she opened the door herself

## Relationships

- **Dasha Pavlov** — Her protector and de facto older sister. Dasha is fiercely loyal to Chandy.
- **Ren Zhao** — Gentle with Chandra; coaxes her out from under the lab table by talking to BiBi.
- **Marty Mendoza** — Quietly covers for her, letting her believe her spell worked.
- **Robi (R081)** — Her robot companion. He calls her "Doctor Chandra."

## Role in the Story

Chandra's apparent lack of technical knowledge is a recurring misdirect — her intuitive framework often leads the team to the right answer (the "pretty arrow" pointing to the control room; calling out to R081). She is the emotional heart of the group and the one least burdened by the weight of what's happened.

## Notes

- Age: **9** confirmed in E1 script. Design sheet said 8; script is canon.
- "BiBi," "BIBI," and "BeeBee" all refer to the same stuffed dragon across scripts. "BiBi" is the E1 spelling and preferred form.
