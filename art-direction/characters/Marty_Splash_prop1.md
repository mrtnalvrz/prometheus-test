**Deviations to flag:**

- **CRITICAL — Energy color:** Marty's holographic energy is rendered in bright red/pink neon. Per the series color language (`art-direction/style-guide.md`): **red/pink = villains**. Hero technology is **teal/cyan**. This must be corrected in the next iteration. The prompt said "red holographic energy" — this needs to be changed to teal in all future Marty prompts. His hacker data streams should be teal/cyan with green code text, not red.
- **Minor — Style drift:** The result leans more anime (closer to *My Hero Academia*) than the target western comic style. The "Invincible animated series" style direction was partially interpreted as anime. Future prompts should strengthen western comic language (see updated prompt below).
