## Creation Date Notes

The following assets have pre-2026 creation dates that need to be confirmed
with the authors before filing. Use the earliest documented version of each:

- All character design sheets (PDFs and JPGs) — confirm year NIBIN/you produced them
- Episode scripts E1–E4 — confirm year first written
- Pitch decks — `DS_teaser final_nov2023.mp4` is dated November 2023; use that as the reference for the pitch materials era
- The series concept itself — 15 years in development; confirm the earliest dated document (script, drawing, or notes) that establishes the characters and world

The git repository commit history (starting 2026) does not reflect original
creation dates — it reflects when assets were imported into this repo.
Original creation dates must be sourced from file metadata, email records,
or prior document versions.
