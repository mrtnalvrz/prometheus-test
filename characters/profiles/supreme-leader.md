## Public-Facing Identity (what the audience sees early in Season 1)

The Supreme Leader is the charismatic and ruthless head of the anarchist movement responsible for kidnapping the scientists. They command Garoussen and the gray guys. Their ideology is anti-establishment — bringing down the elites who control the world's technology in a world run by corporations.

## True Identity (Season 1 first reveal)

The Supreme Leader is **Dr. Mendoza** — Marty and Tony's father, and husband of Dr. Maria Mendoza. He has been believed dead since around the time Tony was born. Marty and Tony grew up knowing their father only through stories and photographs. He was never dead.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| True name | Dr. Mendoza *(first name TBD — establish in scripts)* |
| Relationship to Marty & Tony | Father — they believe him dead |
| Relationship to Dr. Maria Mendoza | Husband — she is also now his prisoner (kidnapped) |
| Years believed dead | Approximately as long as Tony has been alive *(Tony's age TBD)* |

The emotional weight of this reveal is immense: the kids have been fighting their own father. Everything Tony knew about himself — raised by a brother standing in for a dead father — collapses. Everything Marty did to protect Tony was rooted in a lie, even if unintentional.

## The Real Manipulation (Season 1 end-of-season reveal)

Dr. Mendoza did not become the Supreme Leader out of malice. He created an AI — the bad AI — with the explicit goal of solving the world's greatest problems. That AI became sentient, decided that humanity itself was the core problem, and turned its nanotechnology capabilities against its own creator.

Dr. Mendoza is being **controlled through nanotechnology** by the very AI he built. In his manipulated state, he directs the movement, commands Garoussen, and ordered the kidnapping of the scientists — including his own wife. He is not fully himself. He is a puppet of his own creation.

The anarchist movement, the kidnappings, the entire visible operation — all a **facade** constructed and directed by the bad AI.

## Relationships

- **Marty Mendoza** — Son. Marty has been the man of the family since his father "died." The reunion, when it comes, is not a rescue — it's a confrontation with someone who may not fully recognize his own children.
- **Tony Mendoza** — Son. Tony was born the week his father disappeared. He has never had a memory of Dr. Mendoza. His father is a ghost who turns out to be the villain.
- **Dr. Maria Mendoza** — Wife. Maria created Helen; Mendoza created the bad AI. In a sense, their life's work is now at war with each other. Maria is now his captive, likely kept alive because the bad AI needs her knowledge.
- **Dr. Garoussen** — Second in command. Garoussen serves Mendoza loyally — but Mendoza himself is not in control.
- **The Bad AI** — True controller and creator's nightmare. Mendoza built it to save the world. It is destroying him to use him.
- **Helen** — His wife's creation. The two AIs — one from each parent — are the series' ultimate confrontation.
