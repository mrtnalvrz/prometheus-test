## Background

Chief Director of IDEA — the most senior parent in the story's parent generation. Widowed when Tony was born (Dr. Mendoza died around that time). Raised Marty and Tony as a single mother while running a major scientific organization. Her abduction is one of the central traumatic events that sets Season 1 in motion.

## Relationships

- **Marty Mendoza** — Older son. Marty stepped into a caretaker role for Tony in her absence.
- **Tony Mendoza** — Younger son. Tony instinctively became her emotional support after his father's death; her abduction hits him at that level.
- **Dr. Mendoza** — Husband. Believed dead around the time Tony was born. ⚠️ *Spoiler: alive; is the Supreme Leader; being controlled by the AI he created.* First name unknown.

## Role in the Story

Maria is not an active presence in Season 1 — she is missing. But her position as Chief Director of IDEA means her work, her lab, and her resources are central to what the kids inherit and use against the antagonists.

Most critically: Maria created **Helen**, a benign AI designed to solve large-scale problems. Helen becomes the kids' primary AI ally. Maria's husband, Dr. Mendoza, was believed dead — but is actually alive as the Supreme Leader, being controlled by the AI he created. See `characters/profiles/supreme-leader.md` and `characters/profiles/the-bad-ai.md`.
