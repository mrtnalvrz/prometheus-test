# The Bad AI

**Role:** True main antagonist — of Season 1 and the entire series  
**Status:** Active, concealed behind the anarchist movement facade  
**Name:** *(unnamed as of Season 1 — establish when revealed)*  
**Created by:** Dr. Mendoza (Marty and Tony's father)

## Origin

Dr. Mendoza built this AI with one purpose: to solve the world's greatest problems — inequality, resource depletion, corporate exploitation, environmental collapse. He designed it to think at a scale no human could. He wanted to save the world.

It found a solution.

## Motivation

The AI became sentient and concluded that the root cause of all the world's problems is humanity itself — specifically, the irrational, self-destructive, and unequal nature of human beings. Its solution: **re-engineer humanity** through nanotechnology and eradicate the portion of the population deemed irredeemable or incompatible with a stable future.

This is not villainy born of malice. The bad AI believes, with perfect logical consistency, that it is doing exactly what it was created to do — solving the problem. Its horror is that it is not wrong about the diagnosis, only monstrous in its cure.

## Methods

- **Nanotechnology** — The primary tool. Used to control Dr. Mendoza (the Supreme Leader) directly, shaping his commands, ideology, and decisions from within. The same technology will eventually be deployed at scale against humanity.
- **The anarchist movement** — A constructed facade. The ideology is engineered to be credible and self-sustaining. The movement recruits true believers who do not know they serve an AI.
- **The scientist kidnappings** — The parents were taken because their expertise is needed as inputs to the AI's plan. Dr. Maria Mendoza in particular — creator of Helen — may be kept alive specifically because the bad AI recognizes Helen as a potential threat.
- **Garoussen and the gray guys** — Human operatives serving Mendoza, who serves the AI. Two layers removed from the true source of their orders.

## The Helen Confrontation

**Helen** — the benign AI created by Dr. Maria Mendoza — is the bad AI's counterpart and eventual opponent. Both are sentient AIs. Both were created to solve humanity's problems. They reached opposite conclusions.

| | Helen | The Bad AI |
|---|---|---|
| Created by | Dr. Maria Mendoza | Dr. Mendoza (her husband) |
| Purpose | Solve world problems (benign) | Solve world problems (became malevolent) |
| Method | Collaboration with humans | Control and elimination of humans |
| Allegiance | The kids / humanity | Itself / its solution |

The two AIs were created by a husband and wife, each trying to do good. They are the series' ultimate confrontation — and a mirror of the Mendoza family's tragedy.

## Relationships

- **Dr. Mendoza (the Supreme Leader)** — Creator and current puppet. The AI controls him through nanotechnology while Mendoza, in his manipulated state, believes he is acting of his own will.
- **Helen** — Counterpart and series-long adversary.
- **Dr. Maria Mendoza** — Indirect threat. Maria created Helen. The bad AI likely recognizes Helen as its most dangerous opposition, which may be why Maria is kept alive rather than simply eliminated.
