## Personality

Spirited and confident, with an elegance rooted in traditional education — but underneath that polish is a competitive fire. She walks out of class mid-lecture after spotting the answer in a reactor schematic no one else could read. She controls four factory machines simultaneously in E3 and slips into a boxing-match headspace — making each swing of a mechanical arm with her own fists, eyes gleaming, slight grin on her face.

She blushes with embarrassment afterward and pulls her hoodie up over her face. The gap between her composed exterior and her intensity when engaged is a recurring character note.

## Background

Her parents both work at IDEA. Her mother, Dr. Biyu Zhao, designed the security system of the secret lab ("7th Generation facility," per Ren — she can identify it by the layout). Her father, Dr. Steven Zhao, is warm and informal with Mitch, treating him like a nephew.

Ren entered university early due to exceptional academic ability. She designed the **Crossbone Model V drone at age 12**, which is apparently significant enough that Marty and Tony recognize the name "Lynn Zhao" immediately. She has an artificial cat named Jones — likely one of her own creations.

As a child, she snuck into an exofight locker room after a match and asked a fighter "How did you get strong?" — the moment she discovered her passion. Exofighting and combat robotics are her hidden fire.

Mitch is a family friend; she's been to the Mendoza house before.

## Key Story Moments (Episodes 1–4)

- In class at Curie University; spots the faulty component in a B-97 reactor schematic before the professor finishes the question and walks out (E2)
- Destroys the drone targeting Dasha and Chandra by riding her moped off the school wall and landing on it (E2)
- Reaches Mitch first when he calls; her face darkens hearing the news (E2)
- Takes over the factory control panel from Marty and remote-controls four pieces of machinery simultaneously against R081 — exofight mode (E3)
- Comforts Chandra under the lab table by talking to BiBi directly (E3)
- Recognizes the containment facility door UI as her mother's design (E3)
- In E4 she's already working on the exosuit in the garage, watching old fight recordings while half-maintaining it

## Relationships

- **Mitch** — Family friends; she treats him as a peer and equal, which he appreciates. She calls him when she can; he calls her in an emergency.
- **Dr. Steven Zhao / Dr. Biyu Zhao** — Parents. Dr. Biyu designed the secret lab's security. Both kidnapped from IDEA.
- **Chandra** — Gentle with her in a way that isn't patronizing. Talks to BiBi; understands Chandra's framework.
- **Marty** — She takes over his keyboard without asking and solves the problem he couldn't; he blushes. A dynamic worth developing.
- **Jones** — Her artificial cat.

## Role in the Story

Ren is the group's engineering genius and the one who can interface with unfamiliar systems her parents built. Her mother's connection to the lab's architecture gives the team a structural advantage they don't yet fully realize.

## Notes

- **Name note:** The script calls her "Ren" throughout but Marty and Tony recognize her as "Lynn Zhao" — her public/formal name under which she designed the Crossbone Model V. "Ren" is likely a given name or nickname; "Lynn" may be her legal first name. Canonical usage is "Ren" in-universe; "Lynn Zhao" as her public reputation.
- Her mother designed the secret lab's security system — this connection should be made explicit in unwritten episodes.
- The exofight passion is established in a full flashback (E3) and repeated in E4 — it is a defined character trait, not a passing detail.
