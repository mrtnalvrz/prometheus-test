## What This Image Shows

Character design template sheet for the "dark agents" — Garoussen's foot soldiers. Two-view turnaround showing a single template figure (front and back). The figure wears a dark charcoal/black business suit, white dress shirt, dark tie, and black shoes. Distinctive features: pink/red visor-style glasses that obscure the eyes, and a small lightning-bolt or EKG-style symbol on the left lapel. Design sheet notes that hair and skin color vary by individual's race. Pantone color swatches included.

Design sheet metadata: Season 01, Episode 01 "Pilot." ID: DS_villians_extras_darkagents. Notes on sheet: "Hair and skin colors may vary depending on the individual's race. Height: Vary but they must look big (185-200cm)."

**Deviations to flag:**
- Scripts refer to these characters as "grey guys" while this design sheet calls them "dark agents." These refer to the same characters. The canonical label in production materials is "dark agents."
- The suit appears dark navy/charcoal, not grey. The "grey guys" script description may refer to their affect/behavior rather than clothing color, or may be an early working description superseded by this design.

## Production Notes

The visor glasses are the key visual unifier across all individuals — individual skin tone and hair color vary intentionally to show diversity. The lapel symbol appears in colored pages as a consistent gang/faction identifier. In colored pages the visors read as bright red/pink.
