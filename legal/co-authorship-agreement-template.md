## 4. Decision-Making

**Unanimous consent required for:**
- Selling, assigning, or transferring ownership of the Work or any portion of it
- Licensing the Work exclusively to any third party
- Registering the Work with any copyright office
- Signing any agreement with a publisher, studio, distributor, or agent
- Adapting the Work into a new medium (film, game, merchandise, etc.)
- Adding or removing a co-author
- Forming or assigning rights to a studio entity

**Majority consent required for (2 of 3 Authors):**
- Non-exclusive licensing for promotional or educational use
- Hiring contractors or collaborators for the Work
- Approving new creative directions, story arcs, or character changes

**Individual Authors may, without consent of the others:**
- Create materials in furtherance of the Work (scripts, outlines, designs)
  that are subject to this agreement

## 5. Revenue and Expenses

All revenues derived from the Work — including but not limited to licensing
fees, option payments, royalties, merchandise, publishing advances, and
streaming deals — shall be distributed to the Authors in proportion to their
ownership shares as defined in Section 2.

All expenses incurred in developing, registering, or commercializing the Work
shall be agreed upon in advance by unanimous consent and shared in proportion
to ownership shares, unless the Authors agree otherwise in writing.

## 9. Withdrawal or Departure of an Author

If an Author wishes to withdraw from the project:
- They must provide written notice to the other Authors
- Their ownership share in materials created **prior to withdrawal** is retained
- They have no claim to materials created **after** their withdrawal date
- The remaining Authors have the right of first refusal to purchase the
  departing Author's share at fair market value, to be agreed or independently
  appraised
- The departing Author may not license or exploit their share of the Work
  without the consent of the remaining Authors
