## Structural Notes

- Episodes 1–2 function as a two-part pilot — the kidnapping through the lab entrance.
- Episodes 3–4 are a second two-parter — exploring the lab and settling in.
- The Coover/Lamarr investigation thread begins in E4 and runs parallel to the kids' story throughout the season.
- The "Futurum" manifesto is the public face of the antagonists — it should recur in news and police scenes.
- The materializer device's behavior (producing tools specific to the user's expertise) is established for Mitch in E2. Extend this logic to other characters in later episodes.
