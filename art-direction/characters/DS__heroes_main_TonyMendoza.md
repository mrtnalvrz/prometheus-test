## Production Notes

Color palette centers on Pantone 1655C (orange) and Pantone 340C/3395C (green). Tony is deliberately shorter and more compact than his brother Marty — their visual contrast (Tony energetic/bright vs. Marty subdued/cool) is intentional. The green holographic glow in this sheet is unexplained; his primary equipment is drones, not a materializer. May be an early design placeholder.
